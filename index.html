Basic information

  1. There's 10 different styles and types of whistles sold and also in several colors and finishes.


  2. There's a growing community of people who find a passion in the whistles and call themselves whistle-blowers.


  3. This whistle hangs simply on a thick black chain, all in matte black. The size of the whistle is 8cm x 1cm while the chain of it measures 88cm.

The whistle is not just an ordinary whistle. The Falling Whistle is a way to help and rebuild war torn communities in the Congo.

Child soldiers are still a huge problem in the Congo, and the whistles are a way to help stop the children in the Congo from suffering, and giving them opportunities.
